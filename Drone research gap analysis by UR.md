# Drone research gap analysis by UR

**AI-Based 3D Simulation for Drone Flight Dynamics**
**1. Key Limitations Identified in the Paper**
From the methodology, results, and future work sections:
- CNN achieved **78% ****accuracy**, but still shows **high error in yaw and orientation prediction**.
- No **real-time simulation environment** is implemented.
- VR/Unity integration is only suggested as **future work**.
- Dataset includes only **three maneuver patterns** (figure-eight, circular, lazy-eight).
- Model validation is mostly **quantitative (MSE, RMSE)**.
- No **human interaction or immersive pilot training environment**.
- No **real-time decision-making or reinforcement learning**.
- No **high-performance computing optimization**.
**2. Strong Research Gap**
**Gap: Lack of Real-Time Immersive VR-Based Drone Simulation Using AI Models**
**Problem**
The study develops an AI-based drone simulation model but:
- No **VR-based immersive simulation environment**
- No **real-time interaction**
- No **human-in-the-loop simulation**
- No **high-performance computing utilization**
- VR integration is only proposed as future work
The paper clearly states:
Integrating the model with simulation tools such as MATLAB or Unity is future work and qualitative validation is needed.
